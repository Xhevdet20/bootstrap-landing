What is Cameo?

Cameo is an innovative camera app for both IOS and Android that lets you campture the best moments in life either on still photos or video. Cameo have many useful features to enhance the photos and videos with editing tools, filters and effects. And when you're done you can easily share your results with all of your friends and the rest of the world through Instagram.

Caemo has been designed from the bottom up by photography experts and award-winning app developers to make sure you get the best camera app ever created. We are continously improving the app with new features - this is just the beginning.
